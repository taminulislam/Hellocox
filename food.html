Sea Food

Cox's Bazar is very famous for sea Food. There you can find various types of sea food live.
The fishers catch the fish from the deep sea, then they sold it. You can buy from the fishers & also can do curiar from Cox's Bazar.
Palongki Inani, EFC - A Live Fish Restaurant, Mermaid Cafe, Sea Lamp Beach Cafe are the best resturents for sea food

BBQ

Barbecue is a very special iteam to be taste in Cox's Bazar. You can try the fish bbq there.
There are a lots of open shop who are always ready to serve bbq. You can choose one fish what you want, then they will bbq that fish within 30-40 mins.
You can find some restaurents which are special for bbq. Mermaid Beach Resort, Sea Lamp Beach Cafe are two of them.

Indian Food

Spicy, rich, flavourful and diverse are terms that are frequently used to describe Indian food. All these words are apt in describing Indian cuisine, for it is diverse in variety and taste, and is made up from a wide array of regional cuisines throughout various parts of India.
You can find Indian food in Cox's Bazar in some restaurents like: Handi, Koyla, Chopsticks and Chutney and Oasis by Hotel Prime Park.

Chinese Food

Chinese food, rich and colorful, has diversified color, aromatic flavor, and excellent taste as its main features.
To make real Chinese dishes, none of the three characteristics - color, aroma and delicious taste should be excluded.
You can find Chinese food here: Chopsticks and Chutney, Pizzahut Cox, Crab cafe.

Cafe

A café is a type of restaurant which typically serves coffee and tea, in addition to light refreshments such as baked goods or snacks.
You can find more cafe in Cox's Bazar which can help you for your gossip & refreshments.

Sun Dancer Cafe & Restaurant, Salt Bistro And Cafe, Tim Barista, Swiss Cakes & Pastries, Midnight beach cafe are the best cafe shop in Cox's Bazar so far.
